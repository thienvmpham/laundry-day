Self-Service Laundromat in Maribyrnong

Laundry Day Maribyrnong brings a brand-new, modern laundromat experience to one of Melbourne's fastest-growing inner-west suburbs. Located at 103 Rosamond Road, on the corner of Emu Road, we're perfectly positioned for residents in Maribyrnong, Footscray, West Footscray, and Maidstone.

The Maribyrnong area is booming with new apartments, townhouses, and young families — but many homes don't have the space or plumbing for a full-sized washer. Laundry Day solves that with giant 27KG commercial washers that handle everything from your everyday wash to bulky doonas, quilts, and sports gear.

What's Included at Every Visit

  • Free detergent — just bring your clothes
  • Zero card fees — tap and go with no surcharges
  • Giant 27KG washers — fit your biggest loads, doonas, and blankets
  • Open 7 days, 6 AM – 10 PM — early mornings and late evenings

Getting Here

Address: 103 Rosamond Road, Maribyrnong VIC 3032

Landmark: Corner of Emu Road, near Highpoint Shopping Centre

By bus: Route 82 tram runs along Maribyrnong Road, a short walk south. Bus routes 472 and 476 service Rosamond Road and the surrounding area.

By car: Easy access from Rosamond Road or Maribyrnong Road. Free street parking available. Close to Highpoint Shopping Centre and the Maribyrnong River trails.

Nearby suburbs: Footscray, West Footscray, Maidstone, Moonee Ponds, Ascot Vale, Essendon

About Maribyrnong

Maribyrnong is a rapidly developing inner-west suburb sitting along the Maribyrnong River, just 7 km from the Melbourne CBD. With major residential developments, proximity to Highpoint Shopping Centre, and a growing young-professional and family demographic, the area has seen a boom in apartments and townhouses. Laundry Day fills the need for a clean, modern, no-fuss self-service laundry — right in the heart of the neighbourhood.
