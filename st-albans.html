Self-Service Laundromat in St Albans

Laundry Day St Albans brings a fresh, modern laundromat experience to Melbourne's western suburbs. Located at 4/329 Main Road East — next to Monster Chicken and just minutes from St Albans Station — we're the easiest self-service laundry to reach in St Albans, Sunshine, and the surrounding area.

With large families, busy households, and a thriving multicultural community, St Albans deserves a laundromat that makes life easier. Our giant 27KG commercial washers handle everything from your family's weekly wash to bulky blankets, quilts, and doonas — all with free detergent and zero card fees.

What's Included at Every Visit

  • Free detergent — just bring your clothes
  • Zero card fees — tap and go with no surcharges
  • Giant 27KG washers — fit your biggest loads, doonas, and blankets
  • Open 7 days, 6 AM – 10 PM — early mornings and late evenings

Getting Here

Address: 4/329 Main Road East, St Albans VIC 3021

Landmark: Next to Monster Chicken, near St Albans Station

By train: St Albans Station on the Sunbury line is just a 3-minute walk. Exit the station, head south on Main Road East — you'll see us on the left.

By car: Ample street parking along Main Road East. Easy access from the Western Ring Road and Ballarat Road.

Nearby suburbs: Sunshine, Albion, Ardeer, Deer Park, Kings Park, Keilor Downs

About St Albans

St Albans is a bustling, multicultural suburb in Melbourne's west, famous for its Vietnamese restaurants, busy market scene, and strong community spirit. With a mix of family homes and newer apartments — and a growing population around the station precinct — a modern self-service laundromat has been long overdue. Laundry Day brings convenience, quality, and zero hidden fees right to the heart of St Albans.
