Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Information We Collect

Laundry Day is a walk-in laundromat service. We do not require registration, accounts, or personal information to use our machines. Our website does not use cookies or tracking scripts.

Payment Information

Card payments are processed securely through our payment terminal provider. We do not store your card details. All transactions are processed in real time and no payment information is retained on site.

CCTV

Our premises are monitored by CCTV for the safety and security of our customers and equipment. Footage is stored securely and only accessed when required for security purposes or by law enforcement upon valid request.

Third-Party Services

This website uses Google Maps embeds to display our locations. Google may collect usage data in accordance with their own privacy policy. We also use Google Fonts to serve web fonts.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at hello@laundryday.au.
